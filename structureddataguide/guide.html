Structured Data Guide

1.) Identify which structured data fits the page:
BlogPosting: Every blog post needs this

FAQPag: If the page has information on it that could answer a Googled question (this will be the most common because it helps us cover long tail keywords)

LocalBusiness: Every homepage needs this

2.) Copy the template from Google Drive
Here are links to the templates (they'll open in a new tab):
BlogPosting
FAQPage
LocalBusiness

3.) Click on the text editor button.

4.) Scroll to the bottom of the text.

5.) Paste the template into the editor.

6.) Fill out the template with the correct information about the page (only change the text in the quotes)

7.) Update the page after finishing.
