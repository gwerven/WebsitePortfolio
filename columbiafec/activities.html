Activities

With so many ooportunities for fun, you will certainly never be bored at the Columbia FEC. Click the white boxes on the map below to learn more about the activities you can participate in here at the Columbia FEC!

Bowling

We have a 15 lane bowling alley. Pick your favorite color bowling ball, rent your shoes, and join in on the friendly competition. Nearby bowling leagues often host heated tournaments in our alley, meaning you will always be up for a challenge if you play here. Have fun knocking those pins over!

Mini-golf

We have an 18 hole outdoor mini-golf course. Themed after old-timey western civilization, and compelte with a serene, peaceful stream and water fountains, you'll surely focus on the friendly competition of skillfully putting golf balls.

Live Concerts

Our FEC caters not only to our younger patrons, but also treats the adults desiring some vibrant nightlife. We have a full-size indoor concert hall. Singers, particularly of the country genre, frequent the bustling southern city of Columbia for high energy performances. Complete with strobe lights and state-of-the-art acoustic technology, your hair will stand up while vibing out to a performance by the talented artists visting Columbia.

Laser Tag

We have a 1,000 sqaure foot laser tag arena. Our equipment is capable of doing games for groups of up to 30 players simaltaneously. You can battle it out in either free-for-all or team games. Complete with black lights and the nostaligic neon-colored glow-in-the-dark obstacles we have all come to love, you are guarunteed to enjoy an action-packed all out war of laser tag against your friends (or enemies).

Hungry? Pig out at our snack bar. You're sure to love our wide array of selections such as hot-dogs, hamburgers, fries, and our fan-favorite homemade pizza. Wash it down with a fountain drink or one our signature fruit smoothies.
