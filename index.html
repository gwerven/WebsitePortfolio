Advertiso

For a 24-hour hackathon I created both a business model and a mock website focusing on disrupting the international advertising industry with a crowd-sourced web service populated by local advertising opportunities. I used the Coming Soon template by Bootsrapious for its modern design. I chose the Google Maps API for mapping and location services. The project placed 3rd in the competition.

  • Client: York Technical College & Winthrop University Wired Hack 2017
  • Date: April 2017
  • Service: Web Development
